The website for NYU's Pi Delta Psi chapter (ΠΔΨ). I wanted the chapter to have a
proper home online instead of living out of an Instagram bio — somewhere that
explains who we are, introduces the brothers, and tells people interested in
rushing what to expect.

## What it does

- Introduces the fraternity and the chapter's history
- A brothers page so members have a face and a name
- Rush info so prospective members know how to get involved

## How it's built

Built with Next.js and TypeScript and deployed on its own domain. Kept the
design clean and fast so it loads well on a phone, which is where most people
actually open it.
