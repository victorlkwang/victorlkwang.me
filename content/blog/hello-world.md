Welcome to my little corner of the internet. This is the first post on my new site, and I wanted to use it to explain why it exists.

## Why a personal site?

For a long time my "online presence" was scattered across a GitHub profile, a half-finished LinkedIn, and a couple of dead social accounts. I wanted **one place** that I fully own — where I can point people to my work, share what I'm learning, and write without an algorithm deciding who sees it.

## What I'll write about

- **Projects** — deep dives into things I build, what worked, and what broke.
- **Learning notes** — things I figure out that I wish I'd known earlier.
- **Occasional thoughts** — on software, design, and being a student.

## How to add your own posts

Every post is just a Markdown file in `content/blog/`. To publish a new one:

1. Create a file like `content/blog/my-post.md`.
2. Add the front-matter block at the top (`title`, `date`, `excerpt`, `tags`).
3. Write in Markdown below it.
4. Commit and push — Vercel redeploys automatically.

That's it. Thanks for reading, and see you in the next one.
